Program description

A storage/stash for your passwords. Designed to give your passwords as fast as it can by making double click over the list of stored passwords.
Equipped with light security protocols to keep the data secure.
You can use it to store all your passwords, however, its idea is to just be a quick passwords giver.

This program is intended for people who usually log in to their accounts in different PC's all the time to do their work. Passwords Stash and Passwords Stash Lite give a quick solution to log in faster by just promoting a point-and-click on a list. Carrying it on a USB Pendrive.

Notes from author: I have a special love for this program due to its initial difficulty and the challenges it brought to me when it was a baby.

Disclaimer: I am not responsible for any passwords stolen by the usage of this program. By downloading this program, you are aware of the risks of getting your info stolen if you don't take care of your data stored correctly.

Program features
  • Delete data after failing the master password in a settled number of times system
  • Optional Master Password system to keep access limited
  • Function to print all the passwords inside .txt files
  • Double click to copy to clipboard selected passwords
  • Save, edit, and delete passwords info as you wish
  • Additional notes for the passwords
  • Passwords information codified
  • Small search engine
  • Favorite set system

  • Intuitive
